    How do I listen on a new device or after clearing my browser?

    Your access privileges are stored in your local browser to save you the bother of signing in — if you switch browsers or clear your browsing data you may temporarily lose access. If this happens, click Subscribe in the menu, then open Already subscribed? Restore access. Paste your Subscription ID — it starts with I- and is in your PayPal confirmation email, or find it at PayPal → Automatic payments.

    Where can I manage or cancel my PayPal subscription?

    Log in at PayPal → Automatic payments to manage or cancel. Your access continues until the next payment would be due.
