Set up your music site

Three steps. No databases, no command line. Your music lives on your own hosting via FTP — Netlify just runs the player and the API.

1 — Fill in your details

2 minutes

You need your FTP credentials from your hosting provider, and your Netlify site URL.


          

            
          

        

        
      


      
      

        

          
2 — Paste into Netlify

          One-time
        

        

          Go to your Netlify site dashboard, open Site configuration → Environment variables,
          and paste in the values you just copied.
        

        
Netlify → Site configuration → Environment variables → Add variables

        

          After saving, trigger a redeploy so Netlify picks up the new variables
          (Deploys → Trigger deploy).
        

        
      


      
      

        

          
3 — Upload your first track

          Go live
        

        

          Open /insert.html, sign in with your admin password,
          and upload a track. If it appears in the player, you’re done.
        

        
Upload a track → check it plays in /player.html
